Sample Document Previews

See the quality of our AI-generated real estate documents

SAMPLE

PURCHASE AND SALE AGREEMENT

This Purchase and Sale Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into as of [Date], by and between [Seller Name], the "Seller," and [Buyer Name], the "Buyer."

1. PROPERTY DESCRIPTION

The property subject to this Agreement is located at [Property Address] and includes all fixtures, improvements, and appurtenances.

2. PURCHASE PRICE

The purchase price for the property shall be $[Purchase Price], payable as follows: [Deposit, Financing, and Balance details].

3. CLOSING DATE

The closing shall take place on or before [Closing Date], subject to the terms and conditions herein.

SAMPLE

CONTRACT TO LEASE

This Contract to Lease ("Lease Agreement") is made and entered into on [Date] by and between [Landlord Name] ("Landlord") and [Tenant Name] ("Tenant").

1. PROPERTY

The premises leased is located at [Rental Property Address] and will be occupied by the Tenant from [Start Date] to [End Date].

2. RENT PAYMENTS

The monthly rent for the leased property is $[Rent Amount], payable on the [Day] of each month.

3. SECURITY DEPOSIT

The Tenant shall pay a security deposit of $[Deposit Amount] prior to occupancy.

SAMPLE

PROPERTY DISCLOSURE STATEMENT

This Property Disclosure Statement is provided by [Seller Name] regarding the real estate property located at [Property Address].

1. STRUCTURAL CONDITIONS

The Seller states that to the best of their knowledge, there are no structural defects, roof leaks, or foundation issues, except as disclosed herein.

2. ENVIRONMENTAL HAZARDS

The Seller has no knowledge of hazardous materials such as mold, asbestos, or radon gas present on the property, except as noted.

3. UTILITIES & APPLIANCES

All utilities (water, electricity, heating, cooling) and included appliances are in working condition unless stated otherwise.

SAMPLE

MORTGAGE APPROVAL ADDENDUM

This Mortgage Approval Addendum is attached to and made a part of the Purchase and Sale Agreement dated [Date] between [Buyer Name] and [Seller Name].

1. MORTGAGE CONTINGENCY

This Agreement is contingent upon the Buyer obtaining mortgage loan approval in the amount of $[Loan Amount] within [X] days of the contract date.

2. LENDER REQUIREMENTS

The Buyer agrees to promptly apply for financing with a lender and provide necessary documentation for loan approval.

3. FAILURE TO OBTAIN FINANCING

If the Buyer fails to obtain financing within the specified period, the Agreement shall be terminated, and the earnest money deposit shall be refunded.

SAMPLE

RESIDENTIAL RENTAL AGREEMENT

This Rental Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on [Date] between [Landlord Name], the "Landlord," and [Tenant Name], the "Tenant."

1. TERM OF LEASE

The term of the lease begins on [Start Date] and ends on [End Date], unless renewed or terminated under this Agreement.

2. RENT & SECURITY DEPOSIT

The monthly rent is $[Rent Amount], due on the [Day] of each month. A security deposit of $[Deposit Amount] is required before move-in.

3. MAINTENANCE & REPAIRS

The Tenant shall notify the Landlord of any necessary repairs. The Landlord is responsible for structural and appliance maintenance.
